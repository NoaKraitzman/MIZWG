Teaching Philosophy

"I believe that anyone can love mathematics - they merely need a teacher that can light their way."

For me, teaching mathematics means being committed both to the subject matter and my students as individuals. As a researcher, teaching deepens my understanding, helps me maintain a high level of knowledge in the basics of mathematics, and allows me to expose students to ongoing research. I aim to treat each student with respect and consider each classroom as a collection of unique individuals.

I am firmly committed to each student as an individual and hope to give them the best possible learning experience. My main goal, as a teacher, is to provide my students with the tools and confidence to enable them to succeed.
